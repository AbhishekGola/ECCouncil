```markdown
# Google Advanced Search Keywords

1. **Quotes (" ")**: Searches for an exact phrase.
   - Example: `"climate change"`

2. **Dash (-)**: Excludes a word or site.
   - Example: `jaguar -car` (searches for jaguar but excludes results related to cars)

3. **Site (site:)**: Searches within a specific website.
   - Example: `site:wikipedia.org history`

4. **Link (link:)**: Finds pages that link to a specific URL.
   - Example: `link:nytimes.com`

5. **OR**: Searches for either one term or another.
   - Example: `vacation OR holiday`

6. **Asterisk (*)**: Acts as a wildcard placeholder.
   - Example: `best * of 2021`

7. **Range (..) **: Searches for a range of numbers.
   - Example: `camera $50..$100`

8. **Filetype (filetype:)**: Searches for specific file types.
   - Example: `report filetype:pdf`

9. **Intitle (intitle:)**: Searches for pages with a specific word in the title.
   - Example: `intitle:technology`

10. **Allintitle (allintitle:)**: Searches for pages with multiple words in the title.
    - Example: `allintitle:climate change effects`

11. **Inurl (inurl:)**: Searches for pages with a specific word in the URL.
    - Example: `inurl:blog`

12. **Allinurl (allinurl:)**: Searches for pages with multiple words in the URL.
    - Example: `allinurl:climate change`

13. **Intext (intext:)**: Searches for pages with a specific word in the text.
    - Example: `intext:innovation`

14. **Allintext (allintext:)**: Searches for pages with multiple words in the text.
    - Example: `allintext:global warming impact`

15. **Related (related:)**: Finds sites related to a URL.
    - Example: `related:bbc.com`

16. **Cache (cache:)**: Shows the cached version of a page.
    - Example: `cache:cnn.com`

17. **Define (define:)**: Provides definitions of words.
    - Example: `define:artificial intelligence`

18. **AROUND (n)**: Finds pages with terms within a certain number of words of each other.
    - Example: `climate AROUND(3) change`

19. **Info (info:)**: Provides information about a URL.
    - Example: `info:google.com`

20. **Weather (weather:)**: Shows weather information for a specific location.
    - Example: `weather:San Francisco`

21. **Map (map:)**: Shows a map of a specific location.
    - Example: `map:New York City`

22. **Stocks (stocks:)**: Shows stock information for a given ticker symbol.
    - Example: `stocks:AAPL`
```
Using these advanced search operators can help refine and target your searches more effectively on Google.
